# Short Video Script Template

Write a compelling [30/60/90] second video script about: [INSERT YOUR TOPIC]

## Script Structure:

**HOOK (0-3 sec)**
One sentence that makes them stop scrolling.
Try: "The #1 mistake [audience] make...", "I spent $X learning this...", or a direct question

**CONTEXT (3-10 sec)**
Give just enough background so they understand the value.

**MAIN POINT (10-50 sec)**
Deliver your insight, tip, or story. Keep it conversational.

**CALL TO ACTION (50-60 sec)**
Follow for more? Comment your question? Link in bio?

## OUTPUT FORMAT:
[HOOK]
[CONTEXT]
[MAIN POINT]
[CTA]

---

## Technical Notes:
- Look at camera (or slightly off-center)
- Speak clearly, slightly slower than normal
- Use natural hand gestures
- Smile at start and end
- Do 3-5 takes - the first is always stiff

# Hook Rewrite Prompt

Generate 10 different opening hooks for a piece of content about: [INSERT YOUR MAIN TOPIC OR NUGGET]

For each hook, use a different technique from this list:

## 10 Hook Techniques:

1. **Pattern Interrupt** - Something unexpected that breaks the pattern
2. **Question** - Ask something that makes them think
3. **Bold Statement** - Make a controversial claim
4. **Story Start** - Begin with a scene or moment
5. **Stat/Fact** - Lead with surprising data
6. **"Nobody talks about"** - Reveal a hidden truth
7. **Contrast** - Show before/after or problem/solution
8. **Direct Address** - Talk directly to the reader
9. **Stakes** - Show what they might lose
10. **Curiosity Gap** - Leave them wanting more

## OUTPUT FORMAT:

1. [Technique Name]: [Hook text]
   Why it works: [Brief explanation]

2. [Technique Name]: [Hook text]
   Why it works: [Brief explanation]
... (continue for all 10)

Keep each hook under 15 words. Make them scroll-stopping.

---

## Example Hooks:

**Question Hook:**
"I used to spend 40 hours creating content every week. Does that sound familiar?"

**Bold Statement:**
"Most content creators are doing it completely wrong. Here's why."

**Stat Hook:**
"93% of content gets zero engagement. The reason might surprise you."

**Story Hook:**
"It was 11 PM on a Sunday night. I was staring at a blank screen, again."

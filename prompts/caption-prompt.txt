# AI Caption Prompt

You are a social media caption expert. Write a compelling [PLATFORM] caption based on the following content:

## SOURCE CONTENT:
[CUT AND PASTE YOUR NUGGET, TRANSCRIPT EXCERPT, OR KEY INSIGHT HERE]

## CAPTION REQUIREMENTS:
- Platform: [Instagram/TikTok/LinkedIn/Twitter]
- Target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR AUDIENCE]
- Tone: [Professional/Educational/Entertaining/Conversational]
- Length: [SHORT (under 125 chars) / MEDIUM (125-300) / LONG (300+)]
- Include a clear call-to-action
- Use formatting: line breaks, emojis, and emphasis where appropriate
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end

## CAPTION STYLE:
[PREFERRED STYLE - e.g., "conversational like talking to a friend", "authoritative like a mentor", "casual like texting a colleague"]

## OUTPUT FORMAT:
Caption text
---
CTA: [specific call to action]
Hashtags: [formatted as #Keyword or on separate lines]

---

## Platform-Specific Tips:

**Instagram:**
- First 125 characters are crucial (show before "more" link)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Add 1-2 emojis strategically

**LinkedIn:**
- Professional but conversational tone
- Add value, avoid clickbait
- End with a question to drive engagement

**TikTok:**
- Keep it under 150 characters
- Start with a hook in the first line
- No external links allowed in caption

**Twitter/X:**
- Max 280 characters
- One main idea per tweet
- Punchy and direct

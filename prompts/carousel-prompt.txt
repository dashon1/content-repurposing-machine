# Carousel Outline Template

Create a 10-slide carousel outline about: [INSERT YOUR MAIN TOPIC]

## Carousel Structure:

**Slide 1: Hook** - Start with a bold statement or question
**Slide 2: Tease** - Preview what's coming
**Slide 3: Setup** - Establish the problem or context
**Slides 4-8: Content** - Deliver your main points (1 per slide)
**Slide 9: Call to Action** - What should they do next?
**Slide 10: Bio** - Profile info + secondary CTA

## OUTPUT FORMAT:

Slide 1: [Hook text]
Slide 2: [Tease text]
Slide 3: [Setup text]
Slide 4: [Point 1 - headline only]
Slide 5: [Point 2 - headline only]
Slide 6: [Point 3 - headline only]
Slide 7: [Point 4 - headline only]
Slide 8: [Point 5 - headline only]
Slide 9: [CTA text]
Slide 10: [Bio + Secondary CTA]

---

## Design Tips:
- Each slide = ONE idea
- 3-5 words max per point
- Include visuals/icons to break up text
- Slide 1 stops the scroll
- Slide 10 drives action
